How to Play

🃏

The Basics

4 players, 52 cards. You play against West, North, and East over 5 rounds. The highest total score wins.

Cards rank: A K Q J 10 9 8 7 6 5 4 3 2
Ace is highest, 2 is lowest
🎯

Bidding

Before each round, every player bids how many tricks they'll win — minimum 2, maximum 13.

Bid wisely. Make your bid = earn points.
Fall short = lose your bid as penalty.
♠️

Trump & Following Suit

Spades are always trump. You must follow the led suit if you can. If you can't follow, you must play a spade. Only if you have no spades may you throw any card.

Must follow suit → must beat if possible
Can't follow → must play spade (trump)
📊

Scoring

Make your bid exactly or more, and you score your bid. Extra tricks beyond your bid count as +0.1 each.

Bid 4, win 4 → +4.0
Bid 4, win 6 → +4.2
Bid 4, win 3 → −4.0
💡

Tips

Count your spades when bidding — each is likely worth a trick. The dealer rotates each round, giving different positional advantages.

The last player in a trick has a big advantage — they can see all other cards before playing.
